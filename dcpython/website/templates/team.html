Team

Alex Clark is the Executive Director, President and Founder of ZPUGDC, INC./DC PYTHON, a 501 (c) 3 non-profit organization serving the MD, VA and DC Python community since 2008. He is was the Lead Organizer of the Plone Conference 2008 in Washington, D.C., sponsored by ZPUGDC, INC. In addition, Alex has been organizing and running the (formerly) ZPUGDC (now) DC PYTHON user group for over 15 years. He is originally from Baltimore, Maryland and has a B.S. in Computer Science from Loyola University. In 2004, Alex founded and is currently running his own open-source Python software development and consulting firm, ACLARK.NET, LLC. www.aclark.net.

Amy Clark brings many years of professional management, administrative and financial/legal experience to DC Python. She facilitates the smooth and uncomplicated engagement of our services and resources, and she ensures the quality, accuracy and efficiency of all day-to-day tasks related to the running of the organization and to the serving the Python community.
